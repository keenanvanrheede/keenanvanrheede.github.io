More About Me

Hello, dear friends and compassionate souls,

My name is Keenan van Rheede, and I am reaching out to you with a vulnerable heart and a plea for help. For years, I’ve battled health challenges that have tested my resilience, but now I face an even greater struggle—one that threatens my very existence.

The Journey So Far:

At the tender age of 20, doctors implanted a pacemaker in my chest, convinced that I had a heart issue. A decade later, they delivered a devastating blow: I needed a heart transplant. But life has a way of surprising us, doesn’t it? After just nine months of marriage to my beloved Robin-Lee, we discovered the truth: my condition isn’t cardiac—it’s Myofibrillar Myopathy.

Myofibrillar Myopathy:

Myofibrillar Myopathy is a rare, progressive muscle disorder. It affects my skeletal muscles, causing them to weaken and degenerate over time. As the days pass, simple tasks become monumental challenges. Breathing, once effortless, now hangs in the balance. There is no cure, no magic pill to halt its relentless advance.

Our Plea:

Robin-Lee and I are fighting this battle together. We’ve faced uncertainty, shed tears, and clung to hope. But hope alone cannot pay the mounting medical bills. The cost of treatments, consultations, and specialized care is staggering. We’ve exhausted our savings, and the weight of debt threatens to crush us.

How You Can Help:

Your generosity can make a difference. Every rand contributes to my fight against Myofibrillar Myopathy. Here’s how you can support me:

Donate:

No amount is too small; every cent counts.

Spread the Word:

Share our story with your friends, family, and social networks. Awareness is a powerful force, and your voice matters.

Prayers and Encouragement:

Sometimes, a kind word or a heartfelt prayer can lift spirits. Send us your positive energy—we need it now more than ever.

Life is fragile, and our time together is precious. Thank you for being part of my journey—a journey of hope.

With gratitude,
Keenan van Rheede.
